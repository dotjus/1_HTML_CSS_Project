Errektus the Bold

Background Info

Errektus is a Chadian, who are the descendants of the Atlanteans. Errektus is himself a descendant of Kuul-Whip, the Giga-Lord of Atlantis.

Errektus was birthed on a raging battlefield and is the son of a jacked blacksmith. Characterized as chivalric due to his penchant to save bitches damsels.

He is honorable and has a sense of enduring loyalty. Unlike Kuul-Whip, Errektus has a humorous nature, rejecting the brooding and frivolous philosophizing of his ancestor.
